What is a stem & leaf plot?

Also called a stem & leaf display, a stem & leaf plot is a graphical method for displaying quantitative (numerical) data.

Stem & leaf plots are particularly useful when you're dealing with small amounts of data.


Here's a stem & leaf plot example, also called a stem & leaf display:

Test Scores Out Of 100 Points
(25 students)

Scores: 13, 44, 50, 50, 52, 58, 58, 61, 64, 64, 67, 68, 72, 74, 76, 78, 78, 79, 83, 85, 92, 92, 96, 98, 99

      9 | 2 2 6 8 9
      8 | 3 5
      7 | 2 4 6 8 8 9
      6 | 1 4 4 7 8
      5 | 0 0 2 8 8
      4 | 4
      1 | 3

Notice that the test scores are grouped by the "tens" place value. The "stem" is the tens value and the leaves are the "ones" place value.

Stem and leaf plots also enable you to find median and mode.
